I am a mathematics PhD student at the University of Glasgow, where I am funded by a PhD scholarship from the Carnegie Trust for the Universities of Scotland. Before coming to Glasgow, I obtained my undergraduate degree from the University of Edinburgh and completed part III of the mathematical tripos at the University of Cambridge.

My (mathematical) interests are in algebraic number theory, arithmetic statistics, and arithmetic geometry and I enjoy using computational mathematics to aide my work, see my github for any share-worthy code.
