I am a mathematics PhD student at the University of Glasgow, where I am funded by a Carnegie Trust PhD scholarship. Before coming to Glasgow, I attended the University of Edinburgh and completed Part III of the mathematical tripos at the University of Cambridge.

I am interested in algebraic number theory and arithmetic geometry, in particular the statistics and heuristics associated to objects in this area (see, for example, the Cohen-Lenstra heuristics). I enjoy using computational tools to aide my word, see my github for any shareable code.
