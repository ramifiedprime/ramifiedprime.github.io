I am a mathematics PhD student at the University of Glasgow, where I am funded by a PhD scholarship from the Carnegie Trust for the Universities of Scotland. Before coming to Glasgow, I obtained my undergraduate degree from the University of Edinburgh and completed part III of the mathematical tripos at the University of Cambridge.  In October 2022 I will begin a Research Fellowship at the University of Bristol.

I am a co-organiser for the GEARS seminar, and am also an organiser of the next edition of the Young Researchers in Algebraic Number Theory (Y-RANT) Conference.  In October I will be a co-organiser of the Heilbronn Number Theory Seminar.

My (mathematical) interests are in algebraic number theory, arithmetic statistics, and arithmetic geometry and I enjoy using computational mathematics to aide my work, see my github for any share-worthy code.
