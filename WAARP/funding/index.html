Funding

Please note:  participation is by invitation only.

Participants have been contacted via email about the assignment of travel funding.  All participants have been offered hotel accommodation with breakfast included, this will be in Clifton which is a short walk/bus ride from the Fry building.

Please note:  we have been made aware of a scam attempting to contact participants.  All information about accommodation will come directly from the organisers or staff at the host university.
